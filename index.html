The Tajer lighthouse, built in 1876, consists of a large stone building connected by a closed bridge to a monumental iron tower. The lighthouse is located in a century-old pine forest, and is surrounded by a large yard of 400m2. The apartment offers a fantastic view of the Kornati islands, Dugi Otok and the endless open sea. The apartment can accommodate 4 persons with two bedrooms, one bathroom and kitchen with dining area.
